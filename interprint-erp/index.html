Interprint ERP

Interprint is a leading printing and packaging unit. They used a conventional system of noting down and tallying records using Excel sheets, which were then translated to graphs — readable by anyone who wanted them. The collaborative experience was lacking, and so was accountability. All the available systems on the market provided a valid ERP system, but lacked the basic concept of collaboration and information sharing. The system has been implemented for over a year; we A/B tested and arrived at these results.
